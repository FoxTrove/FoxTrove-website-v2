## Deep audit, not shallow automation

Most failed AI projects start with a tool and go hunting for a problem. We invert that.

- We run a multi‑layer audit of your goals, constraints, and existing workflows to uncover the underlying problems AI can truly impact.[1]
- We look beyond surface pain points to root causes—hand‑offs, bottlenecks, incentives, and data gaps.[1]
- We then design a roadmap that specifies *where* AI should be embedded, *how* it should interact with teams, and *what* needs to change for adoption to stick.[1]

## Your AI aggregation and adaptation layer
